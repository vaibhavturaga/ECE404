As a constructor in Formula One, Ferari has a record 16 Constructors' Championships. Their most recent Constructors' Championships was won in 2008. The Team also holds the record for the most Drivers' Championships with 15, won by nine different drivers: Alberto Ascari, Juan Manuel Fangio, Mike Hawthorn, Phil Hill, John Surtees, Niki Lauda, Jody Scheckter, Michael Sschumacher and Kimi Raikkonen. Raikkonen's title in 2007 is the most recent for the team. The 2020 Tuscan Grand Prix marked Ferrari's 1000th Grand Prix in Formula One.
